# 397 Monthly Performance Pack Guide

Monthly packs are generated by `generate397MonthlyPerformancePack` from validated aggregate telemetry contracts.

## Required Sections

- Cohort and journey usage by `journeyPathRef` and `cohortRef`.
- Completion rates and drop-off rates for each NHS App journey.
- Guardrail breach reason codes, not patient-level events.
- Incident, accessibility, and safety issue refs.
- Manifest version, release approval freeze ref, telemetry plan ref, and pack hash.

## Export Rules

- Include aggregate counts, ratios, and refs only.
- Exclude raw JWTs, bearer tokens, `assertedLoginIdentity`, OAuth tokens, grant IDs, patient IDs, subject refs, NHS numbers, and PHI query strings.
- Run `pnpm validate:397-nhs-app-release-controls` before sending a monthly pack to the NHS App team.
- Attach only redaction-safe browser screenshots or traces from `output/playwright`.
