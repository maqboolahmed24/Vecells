Pending to confirmed ladder
  • Review only: no booking outcome yet, all exposures hidden.
  • Booking in progress: duplicate taps blocked, refresh-safe resume.
  • Pending confirmation: provisional receipt only, no booked reassurance.
  • Recovery: reconciliation, route freeze, or identity repair keeps safe context visible.
  • Confirmed: concise booked summary and ready manage or artifact follow-up.
