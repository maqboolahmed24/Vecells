2. Ingest all materially relevant forensic findings. For each finding:

3. Build the master risk taxonomy. At minimum separate:

   - platform-admin dependency / standards hygiene objects

   - likelihood
   - multi-axis impact
   - detectability
   - blast radius

   - the tasks that mitigate or evidence it
   - the gates that it can block

7. Build explicit treatment plans:

   - transfer to later deferred scope where allowed
